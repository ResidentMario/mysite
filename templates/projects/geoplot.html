geoplot is a high-level geospatial plotting library for Python. It builds on top of geopandas and cartopy, and provides an extremely useful list of canned visualizations for your data analytics needs. It comes with extensive documentation, including a gallery page showcasing a variety of interesting and useful plots.

geoplot is easily the largest OSS library I've written, and the one I'm most proud of.

"Making Python classes more modular using mixins" describes one of the challenges of the implementation.
