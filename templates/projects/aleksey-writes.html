aleksey-writes is a search index over all of the technical writing I did in my first few years in industry (2016 through 2020). I built it as a fun little side project while attending my second batch at The Recurse Center.

aleksey-writes lets me grep through my notes on things I've studied in the past. Like, say, cross-entropy:

The frontend is in React. The search service is containerized ElasticSearch. The web scraper (used to parse the plaintext) uses Rust and Bash.
