The New York City Council Committee on Technology held a hearing on Wednesday, 9/21/2016, to discuss open data and open data legislation.

The hearing was backended by a testimony given by Amen Ra Masheriki, New York City's chief analytics officer and head of the Mayor's Office of Data Analytics (and my former boss—I interned there over the summer), and Albert Webber, who heads the DoITT team that runs the New York City open data portal itself.

Further testimonies were given by folks representing BetaNYC, Reinvent Albany, OpenGov, a newly founded privacy advocacy group, and...myself!
